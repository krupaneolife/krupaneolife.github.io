Neolife Teachers Training Course

Become a certified Yoga Teacher from Neolife Wellness

Your first step to start career in ever expanding field of Yoga. Get personal training from Krupa Maam and get first hand experience in teaching yoga.
This is a comprehensive Yoga instructor training, designed in accordance with the 200 hour requirements for registration with Yoga Alliance International. At the end of the course you will be a Certified Yoga Instructor, you can register with Yoga Alliance International. You will be qualified to teach Hatha Yoga.
Syllabus 200 Hour Hatha Yoga Teacher Training 
  •  Principles of Hatha Yoga
  •  Study of Asanas
  • Asana Methodology
  • Relaxation Techniques
  • Study of Pranayama (breathing exercises) & Bandhas
  • Mudras (Yoga in your hands)
  • Kriyas & Shatkarmas
  • The Yogic Diet
  • Esoteric study of yoga            
  • Yoga Philosophy
  • Study of Hatha Yoga
  • Yoga Anatomy
